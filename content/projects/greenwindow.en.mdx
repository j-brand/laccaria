## Overview

Green Window Agency is a creative agency that helps sustainable brands compete in
the market — brand building, campaigns, product development and social media for
green products. Their homepage is a bold, image-led showcase of that work, from
BRLO's alcohol-free beer to Trigema fashion and the GreenTech Festival. I built the
whole site in Webflow, turning the studio's design into a fast, fully responsive
website.

## The challenge

The design was uncompromising: full-bleed imagery, oversized typography and a
portfolio of case studies that each carried their own rhythm. It had to read like a
high-end creative studio on every screen size — and the team needed to keep adding
new work without waiting on a developer.

## The approach

I rebuilt the design one-to-one in Webflow — the full-bleed hero, the alternating
case-study layouts and the responsive work grid — and wired the case studies to a
Webflow CMS collection so the agency can publish new projects themselves. Design and
art direction were by Thomas Bober, Kathrin Schiebler and Maciej Kulakowski; I owned
the build and the responsive behaviour.
