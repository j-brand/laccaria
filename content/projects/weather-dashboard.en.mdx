## Overview

A dashboard that aggregates weather data from multiple sources and renders it on
an interactive map, updating live as new readings arrive.

## The challenge

Readings arrive continuously from several feeds, and the map had to reflect them
instantly without janking or overwhelming the viewer.

## The approach

Live data flows over WebSockets into a client store that batches updates to one
render per animation frame, so the Mapbox layers stay smooth regardless of
volume. The layout is fully responsive.

> Live weather, everywhere on the map, without a single reload.
