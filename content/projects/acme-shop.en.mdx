## Overview

A fast, headless e-commerce platform with server-side rendering, incremental
static regeneration and a Stripe checkout integration.

## Highlights

- Product catalogue with filtering and full-text search
- Cart and checkout via Stripe
- Lighthouse score of 99 on mobile

## Outcome

Page load time was halved compared to the legacy platform and the conversion
rate rose by 18%.
