## Overview

Acme was outgrowing a themed store — slow page loads and a checkout that fought
them. I rebuilt the storefront as a headless Next.js app while keeping their
existing commerce backend for orders and inventory.

## The challenge

The catalogue is large and changes daily, so pages had to be fast without ever
serving a stale price or an out-of-stock product.

## The approach

I used incremental static regeneration for product pages with on-demand
revalidation from backend webhooks, so prices update within seconds while the
page stays static-fast. Checkout runs on Stripe.

> Page load time was halved and conversions followed within the first month.
