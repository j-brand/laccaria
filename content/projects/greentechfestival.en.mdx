## Overview

For several years I maintained the website of the GreenTech Festival — Europe's
largest sustainability event, co-founded by Nico Rosberg. The site ran on WordPress
with a custom theme that I built together with a designer: he owned the design, I
owned the technical implementation.

## The challenge

The editorial team needed to manage complex, recurring content — speakers,
programme, exhibitors, news — on their own, without touching code. And as the
festival grew beyond Berlin to also take place in Los Angeles, Singapore and London,
it needed several standalone location sites that stayed visually consistent under
one shared brand.

## The approach

For the backend I used ACF (Advanced Custom Fields) to build every form and content
block the editors needed, so they could populate each page themselves. As the
festival expanded, I converted the installation into a WordPress multisite: every
location shares the same theme while maintaining its own content separately. Beyond
development, I also produced and published editorial content and trained the team on
how to work with the system.

> One theme, many locations — from the Berlin festival to a worldwide tour, without
> losing the shared brand.
