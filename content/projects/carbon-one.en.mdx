## Overview

C1 Green Chemicals develops a new catalytic process for producing green methanol — a
key building block for defossilising the chemical industry and shipping. carbon.one is
the company's one-page web presence: a single, scrollable story that introduces the
technology, the team and the mission. Thomas Bober and I designed the page together,
and I built it in Webflow as a fast, fully responsive site.

## The challenge

The subject is deeply technical — catalysis, molecules, industrial chemistry — yet the
page had to feel approachable and confident to investors, partners and future hires
alike. Everything had to live on a single page, guiding the reader from a bold headline
claim down through the science to a clear call to action, and hold up on every screen
size.

## The approach

We designed the one-pager as a series of full-width sections with generous typography
and considered motion, then I implemented it pixel-for-pixel in Webflow. Interactions
and scroll animations were built natively in Webflow so the page stays fast and easy for
the team to maintain, and the responsive behaviour was tuned by hand from wide desktop
down to mobile.

> A science-heavy story, told as one bold, scrollable page.
