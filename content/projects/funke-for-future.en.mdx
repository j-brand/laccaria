## Overview

FUNKE For Future is a platform that promotes online courses — both live and
on-demand — around sustainability in companies and the workplace. I took a layout
originally created by another agency and rebuilt it from the ground up in Webflow:
every element built anew, staying inside the brand's fixed colour palette. Courses
live in a Webflow CMS collection, so live and available courses can be published and
updated on their own, and each course is bookable directly on the page through an
Ablefy checkout embedded via iframe. I built the site and actively cared for it
through 2025 and 2026, adding new pages and new courses as the programme grew.

## The challenge

The starting point wasn't a blank canvas: another company had already designed the
layout, and the brand colours were locked. I had to honour that visual direction
while rebuilding every component cleanly in Webflow — and the site couldn't stand
still. New courses, campaign landing pages such as the NRW-funded MITTELSTAND 360°
programme, and the events, podcast and community network all had to be added
regularly, without a developer in the loop for every change.

## The approach

I rebuilt the design element by element in Webflow, keeping strictly within the fixed
palette, and wired the courses to a Webflow CMS collection so live and on-demand
courses could be published, filtered and kept up to date. Booking runs through
Ablefy: each course embeds its Ablefy checkout in an iframe, so visitors book right on
the page instead of being sent elsewhere.

> From a fixed palette to a living course platform — new courses, live and on demand,
> published straight from the CMS and bookable on the page.
